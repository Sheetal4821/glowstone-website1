ABOUT GLOWSTONE

About Glowstone

Glowstone Industries Private Limited is a leading manufacturer and global distributor of premium engineered quartz surfaces. Operating under the brand Glowstone, the company produces high-performance quartz slabs widely used for kitchen countertops, wall cladding, vanity tops, tables, and select flooring applications.

Glowstone serves both domestic and international markets. In India, the brand supplies quartz slabs to kitchen studios, architects, developers, and stone dealerships. Internationally, Glowstone exports to distributors across North America, Europe, and the Middle East, supporting a growing network of fabricators and stone suppliers worldwide.

With over 300 stunning designs and a 25-acre factory producing 180,000 slabs annually, Glowstone operates under two brands: Global Quartz (UK & Europe) and Eternal Surfaces (India)—both reflecting our dedication to craftsmanship and excellence.

MANUFACTURING

Value Chain & Production

The company manages the entire value chain, from raw material sourcing and research to manufacturing, marketing, and logistics. With advanced manufacturing infrastructure and in-house grinding capabilities, Glowstone maintains strict control over quality and consistency.

The company operates a large-scale manufacturing facility in Rajasthan, India, equipped with 3 HR Meze lines and the VEEGOO production line, along with Breton and Keda polishing systems. Raw materials are sourced from quarries just 2 hours away, and our in-house grits plant uses only the purest quartz. Each slab undergoes a 15-step rigorous quality control process before it reaches your space.
