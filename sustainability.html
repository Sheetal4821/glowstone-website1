Sustainability at Glowstone

Solar Power

Our factory relies on solar power for approximately 20% of electricity (19–21%), rising to 22% in summer. We plan to expand from 800 kW to 2000 kW—a 150% increase.

Energy Efficiency

We've replaced conventional bulbs and tubelights with LEDs, achieving an 80% reduction in electricity consumption.

Electric Vehicles

We operate electric forklifts in the factory, prioritising electric over gas-powered alternatives.

Zero Water Discharge

Despite a reservoir of 11.5 lakh litres, we achieve 0% water discharge. Slurry from recycling is repurposed in brickmaking and construction.

Local Sourcing

We source raw materials from quarries just 2 hours away in Rajasthan, significantly reducing our carbon footprint compared to imported sourcing.
