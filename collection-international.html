GLOBAL QUARTZ

Discover Your Signature Surface

Our International Collection is available under the brand Global Quartz across the UK, Europe, and beyond. We export to distributors in North America, Europe, and the Middle East, supporting a growing network of fabricators and stone suppliers worldwide.

Explore a curated selection of colours, patterns, and textures inspired by nature and modern design trends. From bold marble-inspired veining to subtle minimal tones, our surfaces complement every interior vision.

With over 300 stunning designs and a 25-acre factory producing 180,000 slabs annually, we bring craftsmanship and excellence to every corner of the globe.
