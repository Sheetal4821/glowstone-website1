Our Commitment

Sustainability at Glowstone

Solar Power

Our factory relies on solar power for a significant portion of electricity, rising in summer. We are expanding our clean energy capacity to meet growing demand and reduce our carbon footprint.

Energy Efficiency

We have replaced all conventional bulbs and tubelights with LEDs throughout the factory, achieving a major reduction in electricity consumption for lighting.

Electric Vehicles

We operate electric forklifts throughout our factory, prioritising zero-emission electric vehicles over gas-powered alternatives for all internal material handling.

Zero Water Discharge

Our water recycling facility and reservoir ensure we achieve zero discharge. Slurry from the recycling process is repurposed in brickmaking and construction.

Local Sourcing

We source raw materials from quarries in Rajasthan, significantly reducing our carbon footprint compared to imported alternatives.
