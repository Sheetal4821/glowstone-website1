Our Story

History, certifications, and our strategic position

OUR STORY

Our Glorious History

2014–15

Glowstone is Born

We enter the quartz slab manufacturing world, ready to dazzle with elegance and durability. We conquer the UK market and make a name for ourselves.

2016–17

Certified

We achieve ISO and CE certification. Our surfaces become the talk of the town. We step into commercial projects—Hotel Delice and Alaska Resort.

2018–19

R&D on Top

We add a new production line and invest heavily in R&D. Our commitment to innovation earns us a reputation for excellence.

2020–21

We Love Recognitions

Recognised as a Two Star Export House by the Government of India. We expand globally. Our products are NSF and Greenguard certified.

2022–23

We're Growing

We incorporate our third production line and become the only quartz manufacturer in India to be SMETA certified.

2024–25

There's No Stopping Us

We install our 4th line—VEEGOO, build a new office, and enter the Indian market. Standard Chartered and Gartner offices are fitted with our surfaces.
