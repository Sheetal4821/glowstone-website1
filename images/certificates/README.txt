Add your certificate images to this folder with the following filenames:

- ce.jpg          (CE - Conformité Européenne)
- iso.jpg         (ISO - International Organization for Standardization)
- greenguard.jpg  (Greenguard certification)
- nsf.jpg         (NSF - National Sanitation Foundation)
- smeta.jpg       (SMETA - Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit)
- two-star-export.jpg  (Two Star Export House - Government of India)

You can use .png or .webp instead of .jpg if preferred - update the image paths in our-story.html accordingly.

Recommended size: 200x200px or larger (square or rectangular). Images will be displayed at 100x100px.
