Calacatta Series

Calacatta quartz slabs feature bold and flowing veins across bright backgrounds, inspired by the luxurious appearance of Calacatta marble. These premium quartz surfaces create a striking visual statement and are widely used for kitchen countertops, islands, bathroom vanities, and feature walls in high-end interior spaces.
