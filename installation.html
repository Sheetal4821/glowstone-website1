Installation Guide

Five precision steps — from bare space to flawless surface. Scroll through each step and watch the installation unfold in real time.

01
Scroll to explore
Step 01 — Foundation

Preparation
of the Space

The foundation of a flawless installation. Every condition must be met before a single slab is brought in.

  • Complete flooring — correct height alignment for the slab
  • Dry walls — prevents moisture compromising the bond
  • Plumbing & electrical — all utility work completed first
  • Adequate lighting — critical for accurate measurement
02
Step 02 — Precision

Dry Fitting
the Quartz Slab

Place the uncut slab to confirm dimensions match exactly. Seam placement and veining orientation decided here — before any permanent cuts.

  • Accurate sizing — verifies the slab covers every inch
  • Seam placement — positioned at least visible locations
  • Veining orientation — pattern locked in before cutting
  • Sink & cutouts — marked and confirmed
03
Step 03 — Custom Fit

Marking
Cut Lines

Every kitchen has its own angles. Precise cut lines tailor the slab to your exact space — reducing chipping and ensuring a seamless fit.

  • Custom fit — accommodates angles and unusual corners
  • Clean edges — precise marks reduce chipping during the cut
  • Pattern alignment — veining lines up perfectly across seams
04
Step 04 — Bonding

Applying Adhesive
& Positioning

Silicone-based adhesive applied and the slab positioned with care. Even pressure creates a bond designed to endure decades of daily use.

  • Silicone adhesive — flexible, moves with temperature changes
  • Even pressure — uniform spread, no air pockets
  • Precise alignment — flush with cabinets and edges
05
Step 05 — Finishing

Sealing All
Visible Joints

Every joint sealed with high-quality sealant — delivering a finish that blocks moisture, resists mould and makes the surface look seamless.

  • Seamless aesthetics — joints disappear into the surface
  • Moisture barrier — blocks water in kitchens and bathrooms
  • Mould prevention — protects the bond line for years
