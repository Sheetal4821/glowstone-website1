Sustainability

Our Commitment

Sustainability at Glowstone

☀️

Solar Power

Our factory relies on solar panels for a significant portion of electricity, with plans to expand capacity. We are reducing our carbon footprint and dependence on the grid.

💡

Energy Efficiency

We have replaced all conventional lighting with LEDs across the factory, achieving a major reduction in electricity consumption for lighting.

💧

Zero Water Discharge

Our water recycling facility and reservoir ensure we achieve zero discharge. Every drop is recycled and slurry is repurposed into construction materials.

🌿

Local Sourcing

We source raw materials from quarries in Rajasthan, significantly reducing transport emissions compared to imported alternatives.
